how to prepare resume

  1. for fresh graduate: may be education is the biggest highlight
  2. or if u have very prestigious project or achievement. like programming contents 2nd, or robotic contest. avoid highlighting stuffs that are not related to the job requirements. like 23 bf er sathe jogra or tana 21 din kane dhore utbos kora...
  3. dont add. false information. like jeta somporke kono idea nai..seta mote o add korben na.. je ami robot arm banaise...jekhane robot somporke apnar chouddo ghustir kono idea nai...
  4. tobe (amar mawte) ektu exagurate kore likhar dorkar ase. bisoyta jodio halka soromer toy er dorkar ase... ekta example de(balti banaisen just steel er diye ...or find something else)
